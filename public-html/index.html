Who Are We?

Sledge Entertainment Limited is a film production company founded in 2021 with the sole purpose of rendering film/video production services. Our services range from pre-production, production to post production. We are a pool of profesionals, dedicated and innovative individuals specialised in subject matter delivery methodologies in ideation and concept development, scripting, shooting the concept in the most professional way, video editing, audio final mix, color grading,graphics design and final packaging of TV shows, feature films, adverts, documentaries and short films.
